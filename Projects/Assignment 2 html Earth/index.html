Few facts about the world we live in

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known world that supports life. It has a protective atmosphere, abundant water, and a balanced climate that make living conditions possible. Earth formed about 4.5 billion years ago from dust and gas in the early solar system. Over millions of years, it cooled, formed oceans and landmasses, and gradually developed conditions for life to evolve.

About the Earth

  • Mass (weight): 5.97*1024kg
  • Diameter: About 12,742 km
  • Radius: About 6,371 km
  • Surface Area: Around 510 million km²
  • Gravity: 9.8 m/s²
  • Distance from Sun: About 150 million km (1 AU)
  • Notable Moons: Moon

Top 3 Facts About Earth:-

  1. Earth is the only known planet that supports life, thanks to its perfect temperature, atmosphere, and presence of liquid water.
  2. About 71% of Earth is covered with water, but only 3% is fresh water usable for drinking.
  3. Earth has a protective atmosphere and magnetic field that shield us from harmful solar radiation and space particles.
